## Tips for Best Results

### Do's ✅
- Provide specific details about projects and achievements
- Include skills learned and technologies used
- Mention measurable outcomes when possible
- Keep reviews genuine and detailed
- Include context about roles and responsibilities

### Don'ts ❌
- Don't use overly promotional language
- Avoid including personal contact information
- Don't paste irrelevant content
- Avoid extremely short or vague inputs

---

## Sample Combined Input

You can paste all three types of content to get the most comprehensive story:

**What You Provide**:
- 2-3 LinkedIn posts about internship/training experiences
- 3-5 detailed internship reviews
- 2-4 training/certification reviews

**What You Get**:
A cohesive, personalized impact narrative that weaves together all experiences into one professional story highlighting growth, skills, and career impact.

---

## Character Limits

- **Minimum**: At least 100 words combined across all inputs
- **Recommended**: 300-800 words combined for best results
- **Maximum**: No strict limit, but longer inputs may take more processing time
